Rules-Technical Events

Paper Presentation

Time Duration: 8 minutes for Presentation , 2 minutes for asking questions by judges.

Softcopies of the paper has to be sent through mail on or before 22-10-2021.

Hardcopy must contain the same contents as the softcopy.

Time limit should be strictly followed, extension may result in reduction of marks.

Hustle Integrant

Team consists of 3 members only.

Components will be given on the spot.

A circuit will be given on the spot, the components for the circuit will be identify by the teams.

A team with highest identification marks will be considered as the winner.

Circuitrix

Each team can occupy 2 participants.

All components will be provided by the organizers and teams will have to use the supplied components only.

All necessary instructions will be provided to the teams initially.

Techno JAM

This is nothing but a Technical Speech.

Your Speech should be of 1 minute only.

Topic will be given on the spot.

Jury's decision is final.

Quizonics

Totally two rounds-Prelims and final round.

Each team can occupy 2 participants.

Usage of mobile phones is prohibited.

All questions will be of only technical.

Rules- NON-TECHNICAL

On the Floor(Solo Dance)

Track should be of 3 to 4 minutes.

Track should be in pendrive and mobile.

It should be of any concept or theme.

Avoid vulnerable songs and lyrics.

Jury's decision is final.

On the Floor(Group Dance)

Track should be of 5 minutes.

Track should be in pendrive and mobile.

It should be of any concept or theme.

Karaoke Dokie

Connection
